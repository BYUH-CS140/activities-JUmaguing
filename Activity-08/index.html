Pork Adobo

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Dried Bay Leaves
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorn
  • Water
  • Salt

Procedures

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat the pot and put in the marinated pork belly. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour the remaining marinade including the garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour.
  5. Add the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes.
  6. Add salt to taste.
  7. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
