3. Arayat

Arayat is best known for Mount Arayat, an extinct volcano that dominates the townâ€™s landscape and offers hiking trails, picnic areas, and breathtaking views of Central Luzon. The town provides a tranquil escape with its lush forests and natural beauty, attracting hikers, pilgrims, and those seeking a peaceful retreat away from the city.

Mount Arayat is an isolated, potentially active stratovolcano rising to 1,033 meters in the plains of Arayat, Pampanga, and is a prominent natural landmark known for its lush forests, hiking trails, and mystical folklore; it was declared a national park in 1933 and remains a popular destination for nature lovers and adventurers
