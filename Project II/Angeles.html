1. Angeles City

Angeles City is a bustling urban center famous for being the birthplace of sisig, a sizzling pork dish that has become a beloved staple throughout the Philippines. The city is also home to the Clark Freeport Zone, which features Clark International Airport, luxury hotels, casinos, and a variety of entertainment and business establishments. Angeles City is a melting pot of history, culture, and modern attractions, making it a top destination in Pampanga.


Sisig is a Filipino dish made from chopped pig's head and liver, seasoned and served in a sizzling plate.
