5. Lubao

Lubao, located in the southwestern part of Pampanga, is famous for its annual Sampaguita Festival and as the birthplace of two Philippine presidents: Diosdado Macapagal and his daughter, Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo. The town is also known for its scenic river cruises and the Lubao International Balloon and Music Festival.

The Lubao International Balloon and Music Festival, held annually in Lubao, Pampanga, is Southeast Asiaâ€™s largest hot air balloon event, attracting tens of thousands of visitors with its vibrant mass ascensions, creatively designed balloons from around the world, nightly concerts featuring top Filipino artists, and spectacular night glow and fireworks displays. The festival usually takes place in April at Pradera Verde and offers a festive blend of music, food, family activities, and cultural attractions, making it a premier tourism event in the region.
