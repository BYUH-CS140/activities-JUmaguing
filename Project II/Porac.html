4. Porac

Porac is known for its natural attractions and adventure tourism. The town is a gateway to Mount Pinatubo, where visitors can trek to the volcano’s crater lake, and it is also home to SandBox, an adventure park offering zip lines, obstacle courses, and outdoor activities for families and thrill-seekers. Porac’s scenic landscapes and eco-tourism sites make it a favorite spot for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Mount Pinatubo is an active stratovolcano in the Zambales Mountains of Luzon, Philippines, known for its explosive eruption in 1991, which was the second-largest terrestrial eruption of the 20th century and dramatically reshaped the surrounding landscape. Today, its stunning crater lake and unique volcanic terrain attract trekkers and tourists from around the globe
